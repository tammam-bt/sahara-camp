Our Story

About Sahara Camp

Nestled among the rolling dunes of Tembaine, near Douz in the Kebili Governorate of southern Tunisia, Sahara Camp offers an authentic escape into the heart of the Sahara.

For generations, our family has welcomed travelers from across the world with the warmth and generosity of Tuareg and Berber tradition.

Explore the Desert

Desert Experiences

Explore the Sahara with guided activities designed for wonder, adventure, and connection.

Camel Trekking

Ride across the ancient caravan routes at a gentle pace, led by guides who know every dune.

Stargazing

Lie back on the warm sand and let the night sky reveal itself, far from any city light.

4×4 Desert Excursion

Journey deeper into the Grand Erg Oriental, explore hidden oases and dramatic landscapes.

Berber Dinner

Savor a traditional meal prepared over open flames, with couscous, grilled meats, and mint tea.

Sandboarding

Glide down the steep faces of the dunes for an exhilarating rush and unique desert perspective.

Sunrise Hike

Climb the highest dune at dawn and watch the Sahara come alive with color, shadow, and silence.
